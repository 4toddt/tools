What it does. Reads the MASTER FABRICS and MASTER LEATHER sheets, drops marketing-only columns (Married Styles, Notes, Welt, etc.), merges duplicate leather rows by Series Number, splits the rare combo series numbers (Grandview), and detects per-family drop status (peach/teal/purple/salmon row fills) plus per-color drops (red strikethrough lines in the Colors cell).

    
What you get. cover-families.json with a top-level legends map for cryptic codes (wearability, specialty, product line, cleaning), plus extraction-report.json with counts, merges, and any unknown codes worth flagging back to Merchandising.
